Introduction

Pscanner ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information Collection and Use

While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you. Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to:

Types of Data Collected

  • Camera Data: The Service requires access to your device's camera to scan barcodes and QR codes. Image data processed during scanning is handled locally on your device.
  • Storage/Files: The Service may require access to your device's storage to save generated labels, tickets, or to pick images for OCR (Optical Character Recognition).
  • Bluetooth/Nearby Devices: The Service uses Bluetooth permissions to discover and connect to compatible thermal printers for printing labels and tickets.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your mobile device, including the device's unique ID, operating system, and hardware model to ensure compatibility and improve app performance.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to monitor and analyze the use of our Service, or to provide features:

  • Google AdMob: Displays advertisements (free users only). AdMob may use your advertising ID and other device identifiers to provide personalized and non-personalized ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Services: Processes in-app purchases and provides core app functionality. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google ML Kit: Provides on-device text recognition and object detection. See Google ML Kit Privacy.

Data Storage and Security

Your product data and label designs are stored locally on your device. We do not upload your personal label data or scanned content to our own external servers. The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with Personal Data, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.
